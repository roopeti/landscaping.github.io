ABOUT US

We are a landscaping company based in Finland. We specialize in gardens and pathways. We aim to encourage the diversity of nature and discover sustainable methods to preserve greenery and control urban runoff with our innovative yard and landscaping solutions.

Our landscaping professionals create and construct yards of all sizes. Whether it is for a private home, a housing firm, a company, or a public outdoor space, our service team will design the yard of your dreams from beginning to end. In the Uusimaa region, such as in Helsinki, Espoo, and Vantaa, we construct and maintain yards.
